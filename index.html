Seattle Prenatal

Welcome

Welcome to the utilization database for Seattle Prenatal. This is a demonstration site that may be used to browse, update, track, and manage data for a fictitious clinic, Seattle Prenatal.

About Seattle Prenatal

With the mission of ending childhood poverty through comprehensive family services that promote self-sufficiency and healthful living, Seattle Prenatal provides perinatal services, housing support, job training, and social support to 3,500 low income and homeless families annually. Given the breadth of services offered to a growing number of clients, this database driven website has been designed to track and manage client participation in these services.Â Through this site, popular services may to identified, allowing Seattle Prenatal to expand programs in response to community interest.

About this site

This site utilizes the open source relational database management system MariaDB and has been developed with Node.js.
